Best Hikes Around the World

Here are some of the most incredible hikes I aspire to complete:

  1. Everest Base Camp Trek
    • Location: Nepal
    • Distance: 64 km round trip
    • Difficulty: Moderate to Strenuous
    • Duration: 12-14 days
  2. Inca Trail to Machu Picchu
    • Location: Peru
    • Distance: 43 km
    • Difficulty: Moderate
    • Duration: 4 days
  3. Torres del Paine Circuit
    • Location: Patagonia, Chile
    • Distance: 50-80 km
    • Difficulty: Strenuous
    • Duration: 8-10 days
  4. GR20 Trail
    • Location: Corsica, France
    • Distance: 170 km
    • Difficulty: Very Strenuous
    • Duration: 15-16 days
  5. Mount Kilimanjaro
    • Location: Tanzania
    • Distance: 68 km round trip
    • Difficulty: Moderate
    • Duration: 6-8 days

Each of these hikes offers unique landscapes, cultural experiences, and personal challenges. I'm committed to conquering these trails and creating unforgettable memories along the way!
